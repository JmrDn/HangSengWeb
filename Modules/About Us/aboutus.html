About us

Who we are

Welcome to Excellence: Hang Seng Properties Corp.

Founded in 2006 and formally registered, Hang Seng Properties Corp. embarked on a journey to redefine real estate standards. Our inaugural sale in 2012 marked the beginning of a legacy steeped in trust, integrity, and unparalleled service.

Teaming up with PrivateBroker Inc., we present a curated selection of properties embodying Hang Seng Properties Corp.'s essence. Located at Unit 2007, 20th Floor, Philippine AXA Life Centre, 1289 Sen. Gil Puyat Avenue Makati City, our headquarters epitomize our unwavering commitment to excellence and client satisfaction.

Within our inventory, explore a showcase of our current and past projects, each meticulously detailed to reflect our dedication to quality and innovation. From sold-out successes to upcoming opportunities, every venture with Hang Seng Properties Corp. is characterized by professionalism, integrity, and a dedication to exceeding expectations.

Mission and Vision

What are we doing, and why are we doing it

Mission

Dedicate in fostering collaborative relationships that drive innovation and lead to greater achievements and growth in the company. By joining forces with Hangseng, we envision a partnership that not only enhances our respective strengths but also creates a synergy that propels us towards our shared goals.

Vision

We envision a partnership that through combined efforts will result to a fruitful and enduring collaboration that is truly remarkable and bring extraordinary results. A company striving for success and prosperity that lead to endless opportunities and growth to the industry, people and community.
